ChapStick

An ad campaign for ChapStick aimed toward young women in their early twenties not so interested in having the sexiest lips, but wanting to have nice, non-chapped lips. The Ad Campaign was composed of three print components, an online component and an environmental component.

A mock up of an environmental poster displayed at a mall where the ads would be visible to the public and peak interest.

The campaign was focused on showing the efficiency of ChapStick and its ways of healing the chappiest of lips.

An online component was needed to convey the message of the campaign on the internet, but have more of an interactive part to give more of an interest.
